These plants are known for their wow factor and feel for luxury. You will often find these plants in many designer spaces. As for this plants water needs it needs regular waterings durring its growing season biweekly. The soil should be slightly dry to the touch before watering.

For sunshine these these big leafy boys needs bright indirect light to prevent sun burn on the leaves. These guys are picky with their tempeture, they like to be between 65-75 degrees farenheight.

As for the soil and fertalization these plants respond well to a 20-20-20 liquid fertalizer durrinng growing season Just be sure you dilute the furtilizer otherwise this plant is prone to overfertalizing.

Oxalis

These cool plants are known for their deep purple colour and triangular shaped leaves often in groups of three. These guys also bloom flowers every so often. These guys need a very even soil moisture level, especailly when they are just a small plant. Although, eventually when the plant is older they do have a higher drout tollerance.

Oxailias plants are very photosensitive plants, meaning that at night and on cloudy days the leaves will fold inwards and close up, but in bright day light they will open wide to collect lots of light.

These guys will benifit from a slow release and or liquid feritlizer durring growing season. Mixing in some compost might also help this plant out.

Air Plant

These plants are specail because they do not require any sort of soil to grow, they grow in the air (hence their name).

These guys water needs are very interesting, they require frequent watering. Since there is no soil you can't use the regular watering method, you will need to soak them in distiled water for 20-40 minutes every few weeks. Although depending on the specific air plant they might prefer a light misting instead of a soak.

For sunshine since these plants are usually found on other trees or in the shade of rocks they dont like a ton of light they prefer to have bright to medium indirect light.

Since this plant does not require soil and won't do so will if you soil it fertilizing is not really a tast that this plant needs to survive. But, this Plant does sometimes like to be potted in moss and adapt to soil so it is not recomended to put them in soil.
